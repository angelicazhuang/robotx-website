Our Team

Robot X is a hardworking, motivated and productive team. Last year was our first year as a team, so we worked hard and earned the highest rookie seed award. This year we are determined to do even better. The most defining quality of our team is communication, which is a key part of succeeding as a team. Without it, nothing would ever get done and making fatal mistakes would be inevitable. On our team, everybody is willing to do what is necessary to get the job done. We work hard and have fun! Our resources are limited and that is where our sponsors come in. Since we are a very small school, anything and everything would help so please donate. With donations, we can buy better quality materials, better tools, and ultimately build a better robot.
